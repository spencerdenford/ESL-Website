Adult Learning Educational Resources & Technologies

Who are we?

ALERT was founded in 2009 by a selection of former educators and ESL students that felt there was an opportunity to create a more inclusive environment for international students. There was a shared concern that educators did not have the right tools and resources to properly aid them in addressing the needs of their students.

A.L.E.R.T.'s Mission Statement

To provide educators access to the resources and technologies that will greatly improve the educational experience for international students and help them to meet the needs of their international students and contribute to a more inclusive community.
